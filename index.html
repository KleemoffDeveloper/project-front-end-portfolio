Instructions

Intergalactic Buddies is a satirical website for finding the best intergalactic/interdimensional/interspecies buddy in the multiverse. Fill out your user profile information in the section below before searching for a buddy in the site. Type in keywords to pull up related characters and see what their compatibility is towards you. Have fun with your buddies on your multiversal adventures!

How searches work:

Type in keywords that relate to the characters of the Rick & Morty multiverse (ex. name, gender, place of origin).
